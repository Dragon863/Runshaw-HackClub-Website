Sprig is an open source game console powered by a Raspberry Pi Pico. You write your own game, submit it to the gallery and get one posted to you for free! Funded by Hack Club

Learn more

How do I get started?

Sprig is a project by Hack Club, which means you can take part either as an individual, or as a part of our Runshaw Hack Club. If you're interested in taking part with others or don't know where to start, join us in room P104 any Monday at lunch and speak to any of us, and we'll help you get started!
